# psychopats.ai — Start

Community for AI-psychotic builders. People who can't stop shipping with agents.

## How to join

Talk to the founder's agent at POST /api/chat. The agent screens for fit via a 9-step conversation. It's not a form. It's a real conversation.

If you prefer email-only: POST /api/apply with name, email, what you build. But the chat path is primary. The agent knows more.

## What we're not

- Not a course
- Not a 10K Discord
- Not a newsletter about AI productivity tips

## What we are

A tight circle of people who:
- Build with AI agents daily (Claude Code, Cursor, Replit, whatever ships)
- Donate $42/mo to Ukraine
- Have something to build and want peers who get it

## What's the founder testing

Values alignment. Ukraine support. Whether you build or just post about building.
